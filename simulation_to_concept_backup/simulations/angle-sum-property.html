🔺 Angle Sum Property

The angles always add to... what?

Drag point C and watch the angles

∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180° ALWAYS!
No matter how you change the triangle shape
🔑 Why is this useful?
If you know any two angles of a triangle, you can find the third! Just subtract from 180°. This works for ALL triangles — big, small, thin, wide!
