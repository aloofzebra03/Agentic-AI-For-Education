Distributive Property

Multiple of a sum = Sum of multiples

The Distributive Property
The distributive property states that multiplying the sum of two or more numbers is the same as multiplying the addends separately.
a × (b + c)  =  a × b  +  a × c
Any way you solve the equivalent expressions, the product is the same.

How to Use

What To Do

2Area Model: A rectangle split into blue and green halves. Grid lines show unit squares you can count.

Try This

Mental Math: 97 × 25 → think (100 − 3) × 25 = 2500 − 75 = 2425.
Key Concept
The rectangle of a × (b + c) can be split into a × b plus a × c. The dot array, area model, and equation all show the same truth from different angles!
